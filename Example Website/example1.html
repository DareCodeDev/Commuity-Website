About

Founded in collaboration with clinicians, our goal is to promote the applicability of these technologies to improve the quality of life and well-being of children with disabilities and their families in Canada.

Our team

Our team is part of the Musculoskeletal Health, Rehabilitation and Medical Technologies axis of the CHU Sainte-Justine Research Center (CHUSJ). The LaboINSPIRE is located at the Pediatric Rehabilitation Technopôle at the Marie Enfant Rehabilitation Center (CRME) in Montreal, QC.
